User Experience

The user experience aspect of any design is one of the most important things to consider when designing. A bad UX can cause a user to get frustrated quickly and give up on using the interface. This can cause loss of users, loss of sales, and ruin a business. For this project, we will look at an example of a bad UX and how we can fix it!

Bad Design

Here we have an example from Diamond Back Firearms. As you can see in the video, the items are way too big and it takes a whole 20 seconds just to scroll through the pictures. Imagine how long it would take to compare each item!

Good Design

To make it a better design, I shrunk the cards and created a flex box. This is a responsive design that will allow us to look through all the products at once.
